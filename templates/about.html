Company Overview

Streamlining Procurement, Strengthening Partnership


Established in 2000, Safa Star strategically positioned itself in Dubai, recognizing the city's pivotal role in global trade between the Middle East, Asia, and Africa. SAFA STAR is committed to delivering professional and specialized services to all its clients around Globe. Serving as a key supplier of Capital Equipment and Consumables, Safa Star has become a trusted partner for organizations in the UAE, Qatar, Bahrain, Saudi Arabia, and other Gulf countries, while actively engaging in fulfilling export inquiries from diverse markets in Central Asia, Caspian Region, and African Countries. The company's enduring presence and dedication to excellence underscore its role as a valuable contributor to the growth and success of its partners and their businesses throughout the region.
